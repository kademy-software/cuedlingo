With Cuedlingo, the world becomes closer, allowing us to connect with each other through sign language, overcoming all linguistic and cultural limitations. Let's explore and experience the magic of Cuedlingo and open the doors to communication and understanding for everyone.

Our mission

According to statistics, about 1.2 million people have lost the ability to hear and speak. However, the number of deaf and mute people who know how to use sign language is 15% of the total 1.2 million people and that number only accounts for about 180,000 people. So accessing sign language is not simple, this is the problem that needs to change.
